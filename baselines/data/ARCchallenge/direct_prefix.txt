Q: George wants to warm his hands quickly by rubbing them. Which skin surface will produce the most heat?
Answer Choices: (a) dry palms. (b) wet palms. (c) palms covered with oil. (d) palms covered with lotion.
A: The answer is (a).

Q: Which factor will most likely cause a person to develop a fever?
Answer Choices: (a) a leg muscle relaxing after exercise. (b) a bacterial population in the bloodstream. (c) several viral particles on the skin. (d) carbohydrates being digested in the stomach.
A: The answer is (b).

Q: Which change in the state of water particles causes the particles to become arranged in a fixed position?
Answer Choices: (a) boiling. (b) melting. (c) freezing. (d) evaporating.
A: The answer is (c).

Q: When a switch is used in an electrical circuit, the switch can
Answer Choices: (a) cause the charge to build. (b) increase and decrease the voltage. (c) cause the current to change direction. (d) stop and start the flow of current.
A: The answer is (d).
